About

I am a Ph.D. graduate in Computer Science (Shenzhen University) working on trustworthy and privacy-preserving machine learning, federated learning, and probabilistic modeling. I design scalable, secure, and theoretically grounded algorithms with applications in biomedical imaging and data-driven systems.

I received my M.Eng. from South China University of Technology (SCUT) and my Ph.D. from Shenzhen University. I previously worked as a lecturer in Pakistan and have experience in academic research, teaching, and applied ML system development.

Open to: postdoctoral and researcher positions, academic and industrial collaborations in federated/decentralized learning, privacy-preserving systems, and Bayesian modeling.

Research Interests & Current Work

  • Federated and decentralized learning
  • Probabilistic modeling & Gaussian processes

Selected projects

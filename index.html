Public Profile

Short bio and links

Adil Nawaz is a Ph.D. candidate at Shenzhen University specializing in privacy-preserving machine learning, probabilistic modeling, and federated learning. He develops Bayesian and secure distributed learning methods with applications in computer vision and biomedical imaging, and has authored papers in AAAI and IEEE venues. Open to collaborations, postdoctoral opportunities, and research partnerships — adilnawazin@gmail.com.
